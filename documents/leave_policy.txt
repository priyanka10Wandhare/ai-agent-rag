Leave & Work Policy
Organization: Stealth Startup (SSYP Team, LLC)
Document Type: Internal Work Guidelines

1. Work Arrangement
Stealth Startup operates as a remote-first organization. Contractors and employees are responsible for maintaining a stable work environment suitable for remote work.

2. Leave Policy (Contractors)
As an independent contractor:
- Leave is unpaid unless explicitly stated in the contract
- Planned leave should be communicated in advance
- Monthly work hours may be capped based on contract terms

3. Working Hours
Contractors are typically compensated on an hourly basis, capped at a defined monthly limit. Work hours must be tracked using company-approved tools when required.

4. Performance Monitoring
The company may use performance monitoring software (e.g., Time Doctor) to ensure productivity and compliance with contractual obligations.

5. Compliance
Failure to adhere to agreed schedules, reporting, or performance standards may impact contract continuation or compensation reviews.
